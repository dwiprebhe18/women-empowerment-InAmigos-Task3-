Our Mission

Rooted in Purpose. Driven by Change.

Shakti Rising was founded in 2017 with one belief — that every woman, regardless of her background, deserves dignity, opportunity, and the freedom to choose her own path.

We work at the grassroots level across Maharashtra and beyond, delivering programs that create lasting, measurable change in women's lives.

Our Vision
A world where every woman lives free from poverty, discrimination, and violence — fully empowered to lead.
Our Mission
To equip women with education, skills, legal rights, and community networks that transform their lives and communities.
Our Values
Dignity. Courage. Sisterhood. We believe change happens when women support women.

What We Do

Our Programs

Education First
Scholarships, tutoring, and school re-enrollment support for girls who dropped out due to poverty or early marriage.
3,800+ Girls Enrolled
Skills & Livelihood
Vocational training in tailoring, digital literacy, entrepreneurship, and handicrafts leading to sustainable income.
5,200+ Jobs Created
Legal Aid & Rights
Free legal support, counseling, and awareness programs helping women understand and exercise their rights.
1,400+ Cases Supported
Health & Wellness
Maternal health, mental wellness workshops, and hygiene awareness camps reaching rural and tribal communities.
9,000+ Women Reached
